About Me

A lifetime devoted to pastry

Born in France and trained in the finest pastry traditions of Lille and Tourcoing, I have spent over 15 years perfecting the art of viennoiserie, bread, and patisserie across some of London's most respected bakeries and restaurants.


From laminating croissants at dawn to building bespoke celebration cakes, my craft is rooted in precision, quality ingredients, and a deep respect for the process. Every layer tells a story — welcome to mine.

Career Timeline

The journey so far

2008–2012

Trained in France — CAP Cuisine at Lycée Michel Servet, Lille; CAP Pâtisserie at CFMA de Tourcoing. Internships at Brasserie de Paris, Restaurant Nice Espace Tween, and Planckaert Patisserie.

2013–2016

Pastry Chef & Baker at Real Patisserie, Hove — croissants, brioche, puff pastry, quiche, and cakes. Responsible for packaging and dispatch of wholesale products.

2016

Head of Viennoiserie at Lost in Paris, Hove — leading pastry creation, stock control, ordering, and constant development of new pastry lines.

2016–2019

Pastry Chef across The Flour Pot Bakery (Brighton), The Sussex Kitchen, Longboys (Wembley), and ELAN Cafe — broad experience in artisan bread, doughnuts, cakes, and savoury pastry.

2020–2022

Head of Tourier Viennoiserie at Miel Bakery, London — overseeing kitchen operations, lamination production, staff training, stock rotation, and quality standards.

2022–2023

Head Baker at Faran Custom Bakes, London — managing the full kitchen, bespoke orders, team leadership, and creating a more organised production environment.

2023–Today

Viennoiserie Baker at Bread Bread Wholesale, London — high-volume hand-roll viennoiserie production using pastry roll sheeter; lamination, shaping, and scaling.
